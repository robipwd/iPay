Add or Withdraw Money From Any Bank or Card

Add Money

To make any type of transaction, you need money added to your iPay wallet. ‘iPay Add Money’ feature allows you to add money to your iPay wallet from your personal bank account. Link your personal bank account and add the money. It is as simple! What more! Link multiple bank accounts (up to 5 bank accounts & debit/credit cards).

If you want instant money added to your iPay wallet, you can add your Debit or Credit card with charges listed below:

  • 2.5% on Visa Card and Master card
  • 3.5% on Amex card
Withdraw Money

‘iPay Withdraw Money’ feature allows you to transfer money from your iPay Account to your linked bank account. You can withdrawmoney from your iPay wallet into your bank account digitally with no hassle of paper bills or cash such as collecting money from distributors, in-store or online payments from customers or receiving money from sources.

With this service, iPay provides a cashless solution to avoid cash handling risks.
